Why Anandi Gopal

More Than a Preschool. A Home Away from Home.

We believe great early education sets the trajectory for life. Our approach blends global best practices with the warmth of Indian culture.

🎬

Disney-Inspired Learning

Our founder brings Disney's world-class storytelling techniques into everyday lessons — making learning genuinely magical for young minds.

📖

School Admission Prep Built In

Our Junior & Senior KG programs are specifically designed to prepare children for school interviews and admissions in Navi Mumbai.
